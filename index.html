Things everyone forgets they can do
▾
Invoke any aspect — 1 FP: +2 to your roll, +2 to another's, +2 to opposition, or reroll. Any aspect: yours, theirs, scene, consequences.
Stack invokes — Multiple different aspects on one roll. Free invokes stack with paid.
Declare a story detail — 1 FP to make something narratively true. Justify via your aspects.
Compel anyone — 1 FP on any relevant aspect (GM permission). Or compel your own to earn 1 FP.
Concede a conflict — Before dice: exit on your terms. Earn 1 FP + 1 per consequence taken.
Assist someone — Spend your action: +1 to someone's roll. Describe how you help.
Before rolling, ask: What could stop this? What could go wrong? What would be fun if it did?

Your Aspects
How Aspects Work
INVOKE — 1 FP: +2 to roll, +2 to another's, +2 to opposition, or reroll. Different aspect per invoke.
COMPEL — Accept complication, earn 1 FP. Event: [aspect]+[situation] = bad thing happens. Decision: you'd decide [bad idea].
FREE INVOKE — From Create Advantage. No FP cost. Stacks with paid and with each other.
REFUSE COMPEL — Costs 1 FP to say no.
Aspect Types
▾
Character — permanent, on your sheet
Situation — lasts for a scene or until irrelevant
Boost — lasts until invoked once, then gone
Consequence — lasts until recovered. They're aspects too — opponents can invoke them against you
Game — permanent campaign/setting aspects
